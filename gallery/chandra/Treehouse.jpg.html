Moonstone helps the digestive and reproductive systems, assimilates nutrients, eliminates toxins and fluid retention. It is best worn on the heart for the emotions. Light colored opalescent Oligoclase, sometimes called Rainbow Moonstone is a triclinic crystal, like Albite, whose name comes from the Latin, albus, white, which is an alusion to it's color. Judy likes to say she raised millions of dollars for The berkeley Repertory Theater, and so she probably did. After all, she was given her own office from which to dial the patrons, away from the riff-raff: The perenial hippy addicted to pain-killers, the always angry midget of many colors chasing after his acting career, the bossy red-head psychotic borderline-case, and the art teaching artist always out for a good time on the job, and our evil boss, S.D.and A. All of these distracted Judy, hence her own office. It was used by her temporarily, generously shared by the Artistic director at the time, Cary Perloff, now that office is occupied by the great and wonderful Tony Taccone. In my art studio is a tiny skeleton model I bought at the time and brought to our workplace, told everyone it's name was Tony Skeletonne.
