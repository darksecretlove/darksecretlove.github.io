They all are haters of themselves, the girl, women and girls everywhere.
God save us from the "experts" on NPR and other talk shows, the TV news, newspapers, etc. Save us from the Sociologists, Psychiatrists, and therapists---one from Harvard on Talk of the Nation---who said, "they are not monsters, they are all of us." No, they are not all of us. One among them on Forum, a therapist who was a woman got a few things right, mainly that it is misogyny---a hate crime against the girl because she is female. These so-called experts are mostly sleepy; they are complacent in their academic removal from their own subject matter.
I know enough to know that they are monsters, rendered such by criminal child abuse and neglect. Until such an act as was committed by them, they have my sympathy. I also was unwanted, abused and neglected. Not all who were abused commit crimes. Many grow up to be model citizens, even if it takes a more difficult and circuitous path to get themselves there, because of the extreme disadvantage their parents and others put them through.
I scream and cry, I kick and yell, I rant, I rail for the rights of children everywhere to enjoy the protection, guidence, soothing, and encouragement that they deserve from their parents, from us all. But as soon as they cross the line and become perpetrators of terror and pain to others themselves, they no longer have my sympathy. I cease to care about them, I want them tucked safely away from society, I want them thrown away in the slammer. There is no redemption for the monsters who should have been human.
What makes them monsters is that although they are animalistically human, the brain circuitry that would have given them human emotional capacity such as empathy, compassion, properly placed fear and anger----NO, NOT TOWARD ALL WOMEN,THAT JUST AIN"T GONNA CUT IT----is not there; sadly the propensity was lost long ago.
What is needed for us all, after they are safely tucked away is focus on the victim. She needs the attention of us all. We need to know how she is doing, she needs someone who cares and someone she trusts to tell her that people care. People are pulling for her. Everyone wishes her well. Eventually, let it be a world where people will observe her in the kindliest ways, will want to know from her what it is like, what to do, what is wrong, and what can make it better, for her and for all of us. These are the questions I hope for answers to. In the absence of answers, it is at least comforting to know that the right questions are being asked.
Let us begin as a society to find ways to curb the rampant, monsterously unthinking propensity in this world to allow a continuous unfettered birth-rate, worldwide. It is the bringing of unwanted children into this world that is cruel beyond measure. This cruelty begets exponentially greater cruelty, generation to generation. I do not think Hitler and his thugs could have perpetrated the holocaust in a world where child-abuse was not rampant, acceptable, unquestioned. For this girl who suffered this atrocity, the perpetrator thugs were Nazis, hence monsters. I wish her love, and all the best possible for the rest of her life.
